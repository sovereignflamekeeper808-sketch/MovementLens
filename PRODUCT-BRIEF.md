## 3. Five-Layer Product Architecture

### Layer 1 — Optical Recognition
On-device computer vision identifies payment terminals, merchant displays, product tags, and QR surfaces through the lens in real time.

### Layer 2 — Biometric Authentication
Iris pattern recognition and micro-expression analysis provide continuous, passive identity verification. No PIN. No fingerprint. No gesture.

### Layer 3 — Settlement Engine
Movement Pay Core processes the transaction through sovereign clearing rails — multi-currency, multi-jurisdiction, sub-second finality.

### Layer 4 — Intelligence Layer
AI-driven contextual awareness: spending pattern analysis, fraud detection, merchant reputation scoring, and predictive budgeting — all processed on-device.

### Layer 5 — Connectivity Mesh
Secure mesh networking enables peer-to-peer payments between Movement Lens users, offline transaction queuing, and seamless handoff between cellular, Wi-Fi, and satellite backhaul.

## 4. Movement Pay Core Integration

The settlement pipeline from lens to ledger:

```
LOOK → VERIFY → ROUTE → SETTLE → CONFIRM
```

1. **LOOK** — Optical layer identifies the payment target
2. **VERIFY** — Biometric layer authenticates the wearer
3. **ROUTE** — Intelligence layer selects optimal payment rail and currency
4. **SETTLE** — Movement Pay Core executes atomic settlement
5. **CONFIRM** — Haptic/audio confirmation delivered through the frame

Total pipeline latency target: < 800ms from gaze-lock to settlement confirmation.
